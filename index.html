I am a Ph.D. student in the National Laboratory for Parallel and Distributed Processing (PDL) at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), Changsha, China. I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), Hefei, China, in 2018 and my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology, NUDT, in 2020. My co-advisors are Tao Sun, Dongsheng Li, and Xicheng Lu. My research interests include the optimization of machine learning and distributed systems.
