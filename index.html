Xiaoge Deng is a Ph.D. student at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), China. He received the B.S. degree in mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), Hefei, China, in 2018, and received the M.S. degree in computer science from College of Computer Science, NUDT, in 2020. He is co-advised by Tao Sun, Dongsheng Li, and Xicheng Lu. His research interests include optimization for machine learning and distributed systems.
